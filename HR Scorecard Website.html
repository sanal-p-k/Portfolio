Project detail

This project focuses on developing a comprehensive HR scorecard using Tableau to visualize and evaluate key talent management metrics. The HR Scorecard tracks essential KPIs, such as employee retention rates, training effectiveness, performance appraisals, and recruitment efficiency. By leveraging interactive dashboards, the project enables HR leaders to assess workforce productivity, identify areas for improvement, and make data-driven decisions that enhance employee engagement and operational efficiency. Designed for scalability, this tool provides clear, actionable insights that align with strategic HR goals, driving improvements across hiring, development, and retention strategies within the organization.
